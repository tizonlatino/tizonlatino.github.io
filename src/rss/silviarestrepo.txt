SILVIA RESTREPO

Profesora Titular   

1999 - Ph.D (Fitopatología), Universidad de Ciencias Pierre y Marie Curie, Paris VI, Paris, Francia. 
1995 – Diploma de Estudios Avanzados (DEA) (Fitopatología), Universidad de Ciencias Pierre y Marie Curie, Paris VI, Paris, Francia

1992 – Biología, Universidad de los Andes, Bogotá, Colombia.

My group is currently focused on studying oomycete and bacterial plant
pathogens, specifically from the genus Phytophthora and Xanthomonas,
respectively. Recently we have adressed our efforts to understand colombian
populations of Xanthomonas axonopodis pv. manihotis, Phytophthora infestans
and the emergent pathogen Phytophthora betacearum, collected from tree
tomato, a South American solanaceus crop. We use epidemiologycal, population
genetics, biochemichal, molecular, and genomic information to understand the
biology of these economically important pathogens in Colombia and the
interaction with their natural hosts.
